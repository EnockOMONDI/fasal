WHO ARE WE?

Foundation for Arid and Semi Arid Lands (FASAL) was founded in 2006 as a National Non Governmental, non-partisan and not for profit organization, to promote and protect the rights of communities living in the Arid and Semi-Arid Lands in Kenya, and in doing so uplift the standards of living of these communities.We undertake community mobilizationand environment in issues of education, health, environmental protection, peace and security.With time, it has become evident that the issues that we adress straddle the Kenyan borders with its neighbours Ethopia, South Sudan, Uganda and Somalia.We will therefore strive to build linkages with organizations working in ASALS in these countries and ultimately transform into an effective regional organization by the end of this strategic period. charitable organization devoted making the world a better place by to improving the lives of people living in poverty in Northern Kenya by various initiatives such as fostering educational opportunities for children, particularly for disadvantaged girls.
